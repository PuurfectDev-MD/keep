### Keep
Keep is a all in one journalling web app for you to keep track of your life moments and memories through the means of images, audio and videos (coming soon!). You can save memories - with a title and detailed description with multiple pictures to enrich your journal timeline. With that, you can also add voice memos in the voice page where you speak openly about your feeling and mood to reflect upon later.

The website is buid to integrate with a custom made journal device (life_in_pocket) where a esp32 device lets you capture pcitures, record videos and audio and upload to your keep account through an api.
You can use Keep without the device. But to enjoy the full experience of keeping your life in pocket, consider building life_in_pocket or request a pre-made device from me :)

### Why build keep?
The same reason as why I built life_in_pocket:
I have always feared (i still do) that life will just splip by my hands while Im in autopilot. To tackle that fear Im have always been keen in journalling and capturing moments to reminsice later. And building keep and life_in_pocket has allowed me to excatly to do what I want  - capture life!
